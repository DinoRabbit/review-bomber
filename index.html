Welcome to Review Bomber!


Hello, my name is DinoRabbit, a friendly abomination of nature. You can see a picture of me way up in the corner there. In this pursuit, I've been learning about web design, particularly server technologies, javascript, PHP, etc. This site is my first project that I've worked on entirely on my own, all of the programming done exclusively by me. If anyone ever sees this, I hope you like my work.


The goal of this site is to hand power to gamers and to help inform those who may be unaware of the malicious practices of publishers. Here, you'll find the ability to add games from publishers to our library as well as being able to review those games and the ones added by other visitors. The idea is to review bomb the shit out of games implementing greedy practices like microtransactions, P2W elements, malicious DLC, or whatever else you can think of. Publishers can often be major assholes to their customers, and we often don't have much of a voice when trying to speak up about our complaints. The goal of Review Bomber is to let gamers know when a publisher is being a son of a bitch.
